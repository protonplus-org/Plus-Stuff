[SIZE="6"][B][I]First Time Install/Clean Flash[/I][/B][/SIZE]
[SIZE="4"][B][COLOR=rgb(255, 94, 94)]Warning:[/COLOR] Take Full Backup of your Internal Storage[/B]
- Reboot to Fastboot
- Flash boot and vendorboot image
- Reboot to Recovery Mode
- Go to Factory Reset > Format data/factory reset > Factory Reset [B][This will Format the Internal Storage][/B]
- Now go to Apply update > Apply from ADB > adb sideload romfile.zip 
- Reboot to System[/SIZE]

[SIZE="6"][B][I]Update/Dirty Flash[/I][/B][/SIZE]
[SIZE="4"]Use Our [I][B][COLOR=rgb(255, 94, 94)]Inbuilt Updater[/COLOR][/I][/B]
OR
- Reboot to Recovery Mode
- Go to Apply update > Apply from ADB > adb sideload romfile.zip
- Reboot to System [/SIZE]
